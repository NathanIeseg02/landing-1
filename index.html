What is the BMW ?

Bayerische Motoren Werke AG, abbreviated as BMW

All the model of BMW,is a German multinational manufacturer of luxury vehicles and motorcycles headquartered in Munich, Bavaria. The corporation was founded in 1916 as a manufacturer of aircraft engines, which it produced from 1917 until 1918 and again from 1933 to 1945.
